# Privacy Policy for SchoolSaaS Teacher App

Last updated: November 18, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to SchoolSaaS Teacher App! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, teacher credentials
- **Professional Information**: Subject specialties, class schedules, school affiliation
- **Student Information**: Student names, grades, attendance records (as part of teaching duties)
- **Communication Data**: Messages with parents, students, and colleagues

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information**: App usage patterns, feature usage, performance data
- **Location Information**: General location data (if enabled) for school-specific features
- **Log Information**: IP address, browser type, access times

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Services**
   - Manage class schedules and student records
   - Record attendance and grades
   - Communicate with parents and students
   - Access teaching resources and materials

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Personalize app content and features
   - Optimize app performance
   - Develop new features based on usage patterns

3. **Ensure Security**
   - Authenticate user identity
   - Prevent fraudulent activities
   - Maintain system security and integrity

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

### School Administrators
- Class schedules and student progress
- Attendance and grade records
- Professional development data

### Parents and Students
- Academic progress and grades
- Class announcements and schedules
- Direct communication messages

### Service Providers
- Cloud hosting providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
- Analytics providers (Google Analytics)
- Push notification services

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law or to comply with legal processes.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access to student information
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Data Backup**: Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

Student academic records are typically retained for 7 years after graduation or last attendance.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of personal data
- Object to certain data processing
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
